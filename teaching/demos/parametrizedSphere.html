Parametrized Surface: Sphere

(By Thomas Scofield)

\textrm{This app plots a sphere based on settings such as radius. By changing} \newline \textrm{False to True in the addCurvlinearPatch setting, you may add a surface}\newline \textrm{patch depicting how a rectangle in } uv \textrm{-space maps to the surface.}
