Graph of a function of 2 variables constrained by an ellipse

(By Thomas Scofield)

\textrm{This app demonstrates how the equation } x^2 + 4y^2 - 8 = 0\\ \textrm{constrains the values of the objective function } f(x,y) = 10 + xy,\\ \textrm{and displays the two gradients as the ellipse is orbited.}
