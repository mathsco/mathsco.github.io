Elementary Row Operations

(By Thomas Scofield)

\textrm{This app mimics the elementary row operations built into various}\\ \textrm{TI-8x series calculators. The first section of commands, which}\\ \textrm{you should not alter, translate the native zero-indexing of}\\ \textrm{Python into the more prevalent 1-indexing of matrix language.}\\
