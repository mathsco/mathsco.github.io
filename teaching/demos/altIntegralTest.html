Embedding area under f of integral test between two sums

(By Thomas Scofield)

\textrm{Specify your function } f, \textrm{ the viewing window,}\\ {\mathbf r}(t)=x(t)\,{\mathbf i} + y(t)\,{\mathbf j} + z(t)\,{\mathbf k}, \textrm{and which terms to show. If your function is decreasing and}\\ \textrm{positive, the app displays inscribed and circumscribed rectangles.}
